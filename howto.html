How To

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar

  • 2 tablespoons shortening
  • 1 egg

  • 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • ¬Ω teaspoon baking soda
  • ¬º teaspoon salt
  • 1 quart oil for deep frying

Directions

Step 1: Stir together milk and vinegar; let stand for a few minutes until thick.
Step 2: Cream together sugar and shortening in a mixing bowl until smooth. Beat in egg and vanilla until well-blended. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; stir into sugar mixture alternating with milk mixture.
Step 3: Roll dough out on a floured surface to 1/3-inch thickness. Cut into donuts using a donut cutter. Let stand for about 10 minutes.
Step 4: Heat oil in a large, deep skillet to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Step 5: Fry donuts in hot oil in batches until golden, turning over once, about 1 to 2 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
Step 6: Dust with confectioners' sugar while they are still warm and serve.
